  • dc:description "The index type is an attempt to provide a synthetic overview of the aspects described by the Climate Index at hand. "Circulation" indices are synthetic descriptors of circulation patterns, for instance North Atlantic Oscillation (NAO, uses mean sea-level pressure as input) or El Ni√±o Southern Oscillation (ENSO, based on sea-surface temperature anomalies). Indices of "extremes" are used to characterize extreme climate conditions (e.g. the number of icing days, defined as the anual count of days when daily maximum temperature is below 0 degC). "Other" categories exist, so a third miscellaneous category is considered for this property (e.g. heating degree days)." @en
